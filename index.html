Welcome To La Giostra

Whether you are looking for a delicious authentic Italian pasta dish, homemade style pizza, or just a place to grab a refreshing cocktail with family and friends, La Giostra is the place for you. La Giostra is well known for cozy family gatherings, fast and attentive service, wonderful food, and reasonable prices. We pride ourselves on offering you a comfortable setting where you can enjoy authentic Italian cuisine.
